Kaldurg Fort


  • Kaldurg Fort is situated to the east of Palghar, Maharashtra, India in the Sahyadri mountain range of northern Konkan. It is strategically situated at a height of at least 475 meters above sea level[1], giving views of the Arabian Sea and the industrial town of Palghar to the west, as well as views of the Surya River to the east.

  • This rectangular shape of fort can easily be located from a distance. There is no specific historic sign available except fort structure. The fort is divided in upper part and lower part due to a rectangular rock. There are few steps which separate these two parts.

  • It is a popular destination among hikers. The trek starts at Waghoba Khind temple on Palghar - Manor Road.

  • It usually takes 2-3 hours for a one way trekking.

  • The place isn't really a Fort in a true sense,however its natural structure makes it look like one and is certainly the delight that many want to look at, and Tourists certainly enjoy camping Overnight, Enjoying a mesmerizing Sunrise over here and then concluding the day with an equally mesmerizing sunset at Shirgaon Beach!
